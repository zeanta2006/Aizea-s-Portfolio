Welcome

The work of Nigerian Photographer, Oghenekome Aizeanta Oke has appeared in local museums and also around surrounding countries, among other publications. Her recent shoot ''Capturing life in black and white: a grandfathers legacy'', explores an inclusive vision of beauty in a colorless world, trying to tell the story of how humanity is being drained of its once vibrant self. Including a link to purchase these images ensures that visitors unable to come for the next showcase will still be able to see the beauty of the work.

Artist Oghenekome "Aizeanta" has had a strong passion for photography since a very young age. She began her little dream in her final year of secondary school. She hopes to inspire the younger generation to tell stories through their creatives.
